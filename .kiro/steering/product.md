# AmPac Business Capital Product Overview

AmPac Business Capital is a comprehensive lending platform ecosystem designed to connect entrepreneurs with capital and streamline lending operations through AI-powered automation.

## Core Product Components

### Mobile App (`apps/mobile`)
- **Purpose**: React Native (Expo) mobile application for entrepreneurs
- **Key Features**: Loan applications, status tracking, document upload, chat support, calendar integration
- **Target Users**: Small business owners and entrepreneurs seeking capital

### Staff Console (`apps/console`) 
- **Purpose**: React web dashboard for AmPac staff operations
- **Key Features**: Application management, document review, borrower communication, underwriting tools
- **Target Users**: Loan officers, underwriters, and administrative staff

### AI Brain (`apps/brain`)
- **Purpose**: Python-based AI microservice for intelligent automation
- **Key Features**: Document processing (OCR/IDP), AI underwriting assistance, conversational agents, risk analysis
- **Target Users**: System automation and staff augmentation

## Business Model
- Small business lending with focus on SBA 504/7(a) loans
- AI-driven process automation to reduce decision time from weeks to hours
- Integrated borrower experience from application to funding

## Key Differentiators
- AI-powered document processing and fraud detection
- Conversational AI agents for 24/7 borrower support
- Automated underwriting and credit memo generation
- Mobile-first borrower experience with real-time status updates
