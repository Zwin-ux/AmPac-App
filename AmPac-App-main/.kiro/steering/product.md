# Product Overview

AmPac-App is a mobile application for the Asian & Pacific Islander American Chamber of Commerce (AmPac). The app serves as a comprehensive platform for entrepreneurs and AmPac staff to access business support services.

## Core Features

- **User Authentication**: Role-based access for entrepreneurs and AmPac staff
- **Support Hotline**: Direct communication channel for business advice, funding, and mentorship
- **Space Booking**: Room reservation system for meetings and events
- **Loan Applications**: SBA 504 and 7(a) loan application management
- **Business Network**: Ecosystem for connecting entrepreneurs with partners and resources
- **Business Profiles**: Showcase and discover businesses within the AmPac community

## Staff Ecosystem (The Connected Workplace)

- **AmPac Console**: "Mission Control" for lending staff, deep-linking into M365.
- **M365 Integration**:
  - **Outlook**: "Loan Inbox" context and AI-drafted emails.
  - **Teams**: Automated "Deal Rooms" for collaboration.
- **AmPac Brain**: AI Agent layer for document processing (IDP) and policy questions.

## Target Users

- **Entrepreneurs**: Small business owners seeking funding, mentorship, and resources
- **AmPac Staff**: Organization members providing support and managing services

## Development Mode

The app currently runs in development mode with authentication bypassed for faster iteration. Auth flow is commented out in App.tsx but ready for production use.
